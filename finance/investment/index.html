📈 The Power of Compounding

Start Early, Grow Faster

Einstein famously called compound interest the "eighth wonder of the world." The magic lies in time. Even small, regular contributions can grow into a significant nest egg if given decades to compound.

The Rule of 72

To estimate how long it takes to double your money, divide 72 by your annual interest rate. For example, at a 7% return, your investment doubles roughly every 10 years.

Inflation & Reality

While compounding is powerful, remember that purchasing power changes. Professional investors always factor in a 2-3% inflation rate when projecting their future lifestyle needs.
