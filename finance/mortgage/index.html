📚 How to Use This Mortgage Calculator (Manual)

1. Responsible Lending (CCCFA)

Under the Credit Contracts and Consumer Finance Act 2003 (CCCFA), NZ banks must act as responsible lenders. This means they will heavily scrutinize your income and expenses to ensure this monthly repayment does not put you in "substantial hardship."

View CCCFA protections →

2. The Power of "Extra Repayments"

This is the most powerful feature of our calculator. In a standard table mortgage, early years are mostly interest. By adding even $50 a week, you attack the principal balance directly. This compounds over time, often shaving years off your loan term and saving you tens of thousands of dollars.

3. The Official Cash Rate (OCR)

Governed by the Reserve Bank of New Zealand Act 2021, the RBNZ sets the OCR to manage inflation. When the OCR rises, your mortgage interest rate will likely rise. Always use this calculator to stress-test your repayments at a rate 2-3% higher than current offers.

Check current OCR at RBNZ.govt.nz →
